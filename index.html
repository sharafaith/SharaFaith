Most who know me immediately associate me with art. Not in the way that someone might be associated with a hobby or an interest. It's my calling, it's my drive. Visual creation is my passion and my life.

There's nothing that excites or interests me more than creating something with visual appeal. That can mean illustration, graphic design, branding, web design and email. Does it look good? Does it serve its purpose intuitively and effectively? Whatever your "it" is, it's my mission to supply a yes to these questions.

I'm a freelancer with a wide array of experience including graphic/web designer, interactive creative lead, and illustrator for GameStop's corporate office, where I created everything from emails to comic book covers both for in-house applications and co-op video game publishers. Previously, I led the graphics department in UI/UX design for a software consulting firm called Capsher. Freelancing has also given me the opportunity to work on so many cool projects and with so many great people – I've designed logos for bars, software, books, and hockey teams, album covers and t-shirts, posters and character design, and much more.

And just in case you're curious about the rest, here's a snapshot of who Shara is: I'm a compulsive doodler and musing worldbuilder living in the Dallas-Fort Worth area. When I'm not working on artistic endeavors in or out of the office, I enjoy playing video games, doing fitness challenges, and working on costuming and other crafts.

Illustration

I love all forms of creativity, but most people would say that I'm an illustrator first. I've spent my life in its practical entirety with a pencil or tablet pen or paintbrush in my hand. Character design, environmental design, concept art, posters, comics – you name it, I've created it. It's my personal passion. I illustrate both for work and fun, and also have a drive to continually improve through continued education and peer/professional critique.

Graphic Design

I've spent the better part of my professional career as a graphic designer. Logos, posters, t-shirts, brands, and more make up a very significant portion of my work. I seek to make designs that are both aesthetically pleasing and effectively accomplish the goals sought by clients or colleagues.

Web Design & Development

Creating a look and feel for a website or email is another fulfilling avenue of my careers. Here I solve the unique problems that come from creating designs that must serve to meet the needs of the end user and the client, while still looking visually optimum. With a B.S. in Computer Science from Texas A&M University, I'm well-equipped to develop the code to bring these designs to fruition as well. Of all the software development I have done, web coding is my favorite.

UI/UX Design

From full software applications to mobile apps, I've had a hand in many UI/UX designs used in a variety of industries, notably the oil and gas industry and the medical field. I feel very strongly about function as an equal to form. A design is nothing without a good user experience.
